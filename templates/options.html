Black-Scholes Formula
C = S·N(d₁) − K·e−rT·N(d₂)
P = K·e−rT·N(−d₂) − S·N(−d₁)
d₁ = [ln(S/K) + (r + σ²/2)T] / (σ√T)
d₂ = d₁ − σ√T
