How Risk Is Calculated
Value at Risk (VaR)
VaR = Portfolio Value × 1.645 × σ × √(T/252) — the maximum expected loss at 95% confidence over 30 days.
Sharpe Ratio
Sharpe = (Rp − Rf) / σp — measures return per unit of risk. Current 10Y Treasury rate: {{ "%.2f"|format(0.0425 * 100) }}%
